# HVAC Website & FAQ Chatbot

A polished service-business website for **Farooqa Air**, an HVAC and air conditioning company. The project presents a modern landing page with service sections, customer trust content, quote forms, installation visuals, reviews, and a built-in FAQ/intake chatbot for customer enquiries.

This project is designed as a portfolio-ready example of how a local service business can use a professional website and guided chatbot flow to capture leads, answer common questions, and route urgent enquiries.

---

## Project Overview

The website promotes HVAC and air conditioning services for domestic and commercial customers. It includes a conversion-focused landing page and a dedicated chatbot demo page.

The chatbot acts like a simple service intake assistant. It guides visitors through common HVAC enquiry paths such as reporting a fault, requesting a quote, or asking for emergency support. It collects useful context before handing the customer over to a human team member.

---

## Key Features

### Service Business Website

- Modern responsive landing page for an HVAC company
- Hero section with installation imagery and quote call-to-actions
- Service cards for installation, upgrades, servicing, repairs, ventilation, and emergency callouts
- Trust points for domestic installs, commercial projects, and fast response
- Step-by-step process section: survey, quote, installation
- Customer review cards
- Portfolio section linking the main website and chatbot demo
- Contact section with quote request form
- Social/contact buttons styled for WhatsApp, Instagram, and Facebook

### FAQ & Intake Chatbot

- Floating chatbot on the main website
- Separate chatbot demo page at `/chatbot.html`
- Quick action buttons for common user intents
- Handles enquiries for:
  - Reporting a fault
  - Booking a quote
  - Emergency help
  - No cooling
  - Strange noise
  - Leaks or drips
  - Unit not turning on
- Asks whether the unit was fitted by the company
- Captures location/postcode
- Separates urgent jobs from standard visits
- Collects contact details for human follow-up
- Provides a summary response based on the customer’s answers
